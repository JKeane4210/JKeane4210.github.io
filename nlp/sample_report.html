Recap:

MILWAUKEE – MSOE men's soccer was back in front of the home crowd at Viets Field on Friday evening for the first time since they began their season with a 3-1 win over UW-Platteville. Well, the Raiders have done nothing but win since and that didn't change this evening. The Red & White kept the Britons of Albion in check and cruised to a 4-0 win for their fourth straight victory.

RESULT Albion – 0 MSOE RAIDERS – 4

HOW IT HAPPENED

  • With Albion (1-2-1) controlling the tempo in the beginning of Friday night's contest, it seemed like MSOE (4-0) might face some trouble holding on to their win streak. Those thoughts were quickly dismissed midway through the first half though.
  • Freddy Comparato liked scoring so much that he did it twice tonight. The junior opened the action with a goal from Bennett Luttinen in the 33rd minute before finding the back of the net again less than two minutes later thanks to a helper from Connor Dees.
  • Though the shots were virtually even at 10-9 in the second half, the Raiders kept the Britons in check. Nathan Dresen added goal number three just 37 seconds into the second frame and Zach Thompson assisted on a Daniel D'Amato goal in the 67th minute to make it 4-0 good guys.
  • The tandem of Brody Ping and Joey Moore stopped all five shots that came their way as the Raiders win their fourth straight game to open the year.

RAIDER REMARKS

  • This was just the second time the two teams have meet. The Raiders previously fell to the Britons by the score of 1-0 back in September of 2019.
  • MSOE hasn't allowed a goal since the first minute of the opening game of the year. They've outscored their opponents 13-0 over 359 minutes since then.

MSOE STAT TRACKER Shots on Goal – Daniel D'Amato (3) Goals – Freddy Comparato (2) Assists – Three with one Saves – Joey Moore (3)

WHO'S ON DECK The Raiders are scheduled to take on Dubuque in Iowa on Sunday afternoon. Game time is slated for 2:00 pm.
